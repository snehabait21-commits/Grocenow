About GroceNow

Fresh groceries, fair prices, and dependable delivery for homes across Navi Mumbai.

Our story

Groceries made simple, from cart to doorstep

GroceNow is built for busy households that still want fresh fruit, vegetables, dairy, and daily essentials without the hassle of crowded stores. We connect you with curated products, clear pricing in rupees, and a checkout flow designed to be fast on mobile and desktop.

Whether you are stocking the pantry or ordering for the week, our focus is reliability: accurate listings, secure login, and support when you need it. Our mission is to make grocery shopping faster, easier, and more transparent for everyone we serve.

  • Wide range of fresh and packaged groceries
  • Order tracking and account tools for customers
  • Vendor partners working with us to fulfil demand
